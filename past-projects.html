Past Projects

Here are some of my favorite projects

Mira

This is a personal assistant I built that passively listens to your interactions and proactively acts upon tasks and to-dos.

Mira runs on multiple devices and uses an algorithm to select a listening client, then transcribes audio using NoiseReduce and OpenAI Whisper, finally utilizing natural language processing, Mira extracts actionable information and completes tasks in the background using external APIs.

WizViz

WizViz is a multiplayer AR Wizard game. We used MediaPipe Pose to track 33 skeletal landmarks, enabling real-time gestures like heal, attack, and defend.

I completed WizViz at my first hackathon: SpartaHack X, and we were able to win the Interactive Media Track, competing amongst 350+ participants and 100+ projects.

ThinkStack

This project was done through the Imagine Software club, I was chosen to lead a team of 4 developers and 2 designers, building a product for a startup founder.

ThinkStack is a relationship powered note-taking application. Instead of pages and hyperlinks, ThinkStack uses Concepts and Relationships to promote creative freedom.

Crescendo

This is 2024 FIRST Robotics Challenge code, built upon WPILib. As the software lead and robot driver I was a pivotal member of the Robostangs, propelling us to Houston for a chance to play for the World Title.

After placing 16th in the Galileo division, our best placement in half a decade, we were unfortunately not selected for playoffs. This is where my passion for Software Engineering was born.
